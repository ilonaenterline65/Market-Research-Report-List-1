<p><strong>The Automatic Textile Screen Print Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>In-line</li><li>Carousel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic textile screen print machines are categorized primarily into two market types: in-line and carousel. In-line systems operate with a continuous belt that transports fabric through various printing stations, allowing for high-speed production and efficient workflow. Carousel machines, on the other hand, feature a rotating carousel that holds multiple print heads, enabling quick color changes and versatile designs. Both types cater to different production needs, with in-line systems favoring larger volumes and carousels offering greater design flexibility.</p></p>
