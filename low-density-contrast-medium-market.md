<p><p>The low-density contrast medium market encompasses three primary types: X-ray contrast agents, MRI contrast agents, and ultrasound contrast agents. X-ray contrast agents enhance imaging clarity during radiographic procedures by improving visibility of blood vessels and organs. MRI contrast agents, typically based on gadolinium, enhance magnetic resonance images by altering the magnetic properties of tissues. Ultrasound contrast agents improve the visualization of blood flow and organ structures during ultrasound examinations, facilitating accurate diagnostics and treatment planning across diverse medical applications.</p></p>
