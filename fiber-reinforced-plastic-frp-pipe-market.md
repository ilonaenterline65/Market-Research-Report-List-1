<p><p>The Fiber Reinforced Plastic (FRP) pipe market is primarily categorized into three types: Glass Reinforced Plastic (GRP), Glass Reinforced Epoxy (GRE), and Glass Reinforced Vinylester (GRV). GRP pipes use fiberglass and thermosetting resins, offering high strength and corrosion resistance, making them ideal for water and wastewater applications. GRE pipes, incorporating epoxy resins, provide superior chemical resistance and are suitable for oil and gas industries. GRV pipes, using vinylester resins, balance strength and durability, making them versatile for various industrial applications.</p></p>
