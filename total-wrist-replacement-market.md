<p><p>The total wrist replacement market comprises two main types: total wrist replacement and total wrist fusion. Total wrist replacement involves the surgical implantation of an artificial joint to restore mobility and alleviate pain in patients with severe arthritis or joint damage. Conversely, total wrist fusion is a procedure that permanently anchors the wrist bones together, eliminating motion to provide stability and pain relief. Both options cater to patients seeking solutions for wrist conditions, with varying benefits and adaptability based on individual needs.</p></p>
