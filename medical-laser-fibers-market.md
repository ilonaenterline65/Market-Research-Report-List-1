<p><p>The medical laser fibers market consists of two primary types: reusable and disposable laser fibers. Reusable laser fibers are designed for multiple uses, undergoing sterilization between procedures, making them cost-effective for healthcare facilities that perform numerous laser surgeries. In contrast, disposable laser fibers are intended for single-use applications, minimizing the risk of cross-contamination and enhancing patient safety. Both types cater to varying surgical needs and preferences, with growing demand driven by advancements in laser technology and increasing minimally invasive procedures.</p></p>
