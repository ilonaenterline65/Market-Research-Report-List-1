<p><p>The switchable glass film market is categorized into self-adhesive and non-adhesive films. Self-adhesive films are designed for easy application, allowing users to stick them directly onto glass surfaces without additional adhesives or installations, making them ideal for retrofitting. In contrast, non-adhesive films require a more complex installation process, often involving specialized adhesives or professional installation. Each type serves different needs, with self-adhesive films appealing to DIY projects and non-adhesive films catering to commercial applications requiring more durability and customization.</p></p>
