<p><ul><li>Series Battery Pack</li><li>Parallel Battery Pack</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 18650 battery pack market is mainly divided into series and parallel configurations. A series battery pack connects multiple cells end-to-end, increasing voltage while keeping capacity the same, making it suitable for high-voltage applications. Conversely, a parallel battery pack involves connecting cells side-by-side, which enhances total capacity while maintaining voltage, ideal for extending runtime. Both configurations have distinct advantages, catering to various industries such as electronics, electric vehicles, and renewable energy, based on power requirements and energy storage needs.</p></p>
