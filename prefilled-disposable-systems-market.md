<p><strong>The Prefilled Disposable Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Simple Chamber</li><li>Dual Chamber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Prefilled disposable systems include simple chamber and dual chamber types. Simple chamber systems consist of a single compartment containing the medication, providing ease of use and immediate delivery. In contrast, dual chamber systems feature two separate compartments that store the drug and diluent separately, allowing for mixed dosing just before use. This design enhances stability and extends shelf life for sensitive medications. Both types cater to different clinical needs, improving patient compliance and convenience in medication administration.</p></p>
