<p><p>The roach bait trap market comprises three main types: bait stations, granular baits, and gel baits. Bait stations are enclosed units that attract cockroaches while preventing access to children or pets. Granular baits consist of small particles that can be spread in areas infested with roaches, effectively luring them. Gel baits are viscous substances applied in targeted spots, providing an attractive food source for roaches and facilitating their elimination. Each type serves specific needs in pest control strategies.</p></p>
