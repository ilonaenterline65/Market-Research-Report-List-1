<p><strong>The Propylene Oxide Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The Propylene Oxide (PO) market is segmented by production processes: Chlorohydrin, SM/PO, and HPPO. The **Chlorohydrin Process** is a traditional method using chlorine and lime, but it generates significant waste. The **SM/PO (Styrene Monomer/Propylene Oxide)** process co-produces styrene monomer, offering cost efficiency but relying on styrene demand. The **HPPO (Hydrogen Peroxide to Propylene Oxide)** method uses hydrogen peroxide, producing fewer byproducts and being more environmentally friendly, making it increasingly popular for sustainable production. Each method caters to varying cost, efficiency, and environmental needs.</p></p>
