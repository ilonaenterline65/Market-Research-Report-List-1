<p><p>The Brake Master Pump market primarily consists of two types: vacuum pumps and hydraulic pumps. Vacuum pumps utilize negative pressure to assist in brake operation, enhancing system responsiveness and performance, especially in smaller vehicles. On the other hand, hydraulic pumps rely on fluid pressure to transmit force from the brake pedal to the brake mechanism, providing reliable stopping power in various vehicle types. Both types are crucial for effective braking systems, catering to distinct automotive needs and configurations.</p></p>
