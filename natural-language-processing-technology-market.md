<p><ul><li>Natural Language Understanding</li><li>Natural Language Generation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Natural Language Processing (NLP) technology encompasses two main market types: Natural Language Understanding (NLU) and Natural Language Generation (NLG). NLU focuses on enabling machines to comprehend and interpret human language, facilitating tasks like sentiment analysis and intent recognition. In contrast, NLG involves the automatic generation of coherent and contextually relevant text from structured data, often used for creating reports, summaries, or conversational agents. Together, these technologies enhance communication between humans and machines, driving advancements in various industries.</p></p>
