<p><strong>The Infant Resuscitators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-inflating Resuscitator</li><li>Flow-inflating Resuscitator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infant resuscitators market comprises two main types: self-inflating resuscitators and flow-inflating resuscitators. Self-inflating resuscitators are portable devices that automatically refill with air after each squeeze, making them ideal for emergency situations. Flow-inflating resuscitators require a continuous flow of oxygen or air to function, providing more precise control over ventilation and oxygen delivery. Both types are crucial for neonatal care, ensuring effective respiratory support for newborns facing critical health challenges during delivery or immediate postnatal periods.</p></p>
