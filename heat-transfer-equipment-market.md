<p><p>The heat transfer equipment market includes several key types: Shell & Tube, Plate, Fin Type, and Air Cooled systems. Shell & Tube units consist of a series of tubes for transferring heat between fluids, while Plate heat exchangers use thin plates to achieve efficient thermal transfer. Fin Type exchangers enhance heat transfer by increasing surface area through fins, suitable for air-to-fluid applications. Air Cooled devices utilize ambient air to cool fluids, offering energy-efficient solutions in various industrial processes.</p></p>
