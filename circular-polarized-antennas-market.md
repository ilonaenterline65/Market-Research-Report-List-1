<p><p>Circular polarized antennas transmit and receive electromagnetic waves with a rotating electric field, enhancing signal reliability in diverse orientations. They are categorized into Left-Handed Circular Polarization (LHCP) and Right-Handed Circular Polarization (RHCP) based on the direction of rotation. LHCP antennas rotate counterclockwise, while RHCP antennas rotate clockwise. These types are used in applications like satellite communication, GPS, and wireless systems, where polarization alignment is critical. The market for both LHCP and RHCP antennas is driven by demand for improved signal performance and compatibility in complex environments.</p></p>
