<p><strong>The HIV Test Kits Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Antibody Tests</li><li>RNA (viral load) Test</li><li>Antibody-antigen Test</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The HIV test kits market comprises three primary types: Antibody Tests, RNA (viral load) Tests, and Antibody-Antigen Tests. Antibody Tests detect the presence of antibodies produced in response to HIV, offering a reliable indication of infection. RNA Tests directly measure the viral load in the bloodstream, providing early detection of HIV. Antibody-Antigen Tests identify both antibodies and the p24 antigen, enabling earlier diagnosis compared to antibody-only tests. Each type serves distinct clinical needs in HIV screening and diagnosis.</p></p>
