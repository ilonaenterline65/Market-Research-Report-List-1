<p><ul><li>Tubed Fluid Coils</li><li>Finned Fluid Coils</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fluid coils market primarily consists of two types: tubed fluid coils and finned fluid coils. Tubed fluid coils feature simple, cylindrical tubes, allowing for efficient heat transfer and fluid circulation in applications like heating and cooling systems. Finned fluid coils, on the other hand, incorporate extended surfaces or fins, enhancing heat exchange capabilities by increasing the surface area exposed to the environment. Both types are essential in HVAC systems, refrigeration, and industrial processes, catering to diverse thermal management needs.</p></p>
