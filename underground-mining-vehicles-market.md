<p><strong>The Underground Mining Vehicles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Longwall</li><li>Room and Pillar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The underground mining vehicles market is primarily divided into two main types: longwall and room and pillar mining. Longwall mining involves the use of a continuous miner to extract coal or minerals along a long face, with the surrounding rock supported by hydraulic props. In contrast, room and pillar mining involves mining large blocks of material while leaving pillars of material to support the roof. Both methods require specialized vehicles for tasks like transportation, drilling, and hauling materials, catering to their specific operational needs.</p></p>
