<p><p>The Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) Contrast Media market is primarily divided into iodine-based and barium sulfate compounds. Iodine-based contrast agents enhance the visibility of blood vessels and tissues during imaging, facilitating the detection of abnormalities. Barium sulfate compounds, primarily used in gastrointestinal studies, improve the contrast of the digestive system by coating the intestinal lining. Both types of agents play a crucial role in providing clearer images, thereby aiding in accurate diagnoses and treatment planning in medical practice.</p></p>
