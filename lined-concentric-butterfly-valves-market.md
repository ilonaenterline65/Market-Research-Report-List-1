<p><p>Lined concentric butterfly valves are classified based on their lining materials, which enhance their corrosion resistance and chemical compatibility. PFA (Perfluoroalkoxy) lined valves are ideal for high-temperature and aggressive chemical environments. PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene) lined valves offer excellent non-stick properties and resistance to a broad range of chemicals. FEP (Fluorinated Ethylene Propylene) lined valves combine good temperature resistance with superior chemical stability. Other linings may include various polymers or elastomers tailored for specific applications, expanding market versatility.</p></p>
