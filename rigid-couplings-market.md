<p><ul><li>Sleeve</li><li>Flanged</li><li>Clamped</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rigid couplings market consists of three primary types: sleeve, flanged, and clamped couplings. Sleeve couplings are cylindrical components that join two shafts end-to-end, providing strong alignment and torque transfer. Flanged couplings feature a flange connection at each end, allowing for easy assembly and disassembly, making them suitable for high-torque applications. Clamped couplings use a clamping mechanism to secure shafts together, offering flexibility in alignment and accommodating slight variations in shaft dimensions, thus enhancing operational efficiency.</p></p>
