<p><p>Plunger hydraulic cylinders are categorized into two main market types: single function and double function. Single function cylinders are designed for one-directional motion, typically extending or retracting under hydraulic pressure, making them ideal for applications requiring straightforward push or pull operations. In contrast, double function cylinders provide both extension and retraction capabilities, offering more versatility by allowing controlled movement in both directions. This distinction influences their application in various industries such as construction, manufacturing, and automotive.</p></p>
