<p><p>Dental bridges are prosthetic devices used to replace missing teeth, effectively bridging the gap created by one or more absent teeth. The growing demand for dental bridges is driven by an increasing prevalence of dental issues and an aging population that requires restorative dental care.</p><p>The Dental Bridges Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.8% during the forecast period. Digital dentistry, including 3D printing and computer-aided design, is also emerging as a significant trend, enhancing the customization and precision of dental bridges.
