<p><p>The silicone rubber flexible heaters market comprises two primary types: single-layer and double-layer silicone rubber heaters. Single-layer heaters feature a thin, continuous layer of silicone rubber, providing lightweight, flexible heating solutions for applications with space constraints. In contrast, double-layer heaters consist of two silicone rubber layers, offering enhanced durability and heat distribution, making them suitable for high-temperature environments and demanding applications. Both types are widely used in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and medical devices, benefiting from their versatility and efficiency.</p></p>
