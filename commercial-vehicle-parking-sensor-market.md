<p><p>The commercial vehicle parking sensor market consists of two primary types: forward sensors and rear view sensors. Forward sensors are designed to detect obstacles in front of the vehicle, assisting drivers during tight maneuvers and improving safety. Rear view sensors focus on the area behind the vehicle, alerting drivers to potential hazards when reversing. Both types enhance spatial awareness, reduce the risk of collisions, and are increasingly integrated into modern commercial vehicles for improved operational efficiency and safety.</p></p>
