<p><p>The flexible extraction arm market consists of three main types: wall-mounted, ceiling-mounted, and bench-top models. Wall-mounted arms are fixed to walls, optimizing space and providing targeted extraction in proximity to workstations. Ceiling-mounted arms, suspended from above, allow for greater range and movement, effectively capturing airborne contaminants in large areas. Bench-top extraction arms are designed for desktop use, offering portability and flexibility for smaller spaces while ensuring effective fume and particle capture during various tasks.</p></p>
