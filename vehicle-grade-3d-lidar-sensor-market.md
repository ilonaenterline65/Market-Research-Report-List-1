<p><p>The Vehicle Grade 3D LiDAR Sensor market is primarily segmented into two types: Mechanical LiDAR and Solid State LiDAR. Mechanical LiDAR uses rotating components to capture detailed 3D environmental data, offering high resolution and extensive range, making it suitable for complex routes and urban environments. In contrast, Solid State LiDAR employs fixed, non-moving components, providing a more compact and robust solution, typically at a lower cost and with greater durability, making it ideal for mass-produced vehicles and advanced driver-assistance systems.</p></p>
