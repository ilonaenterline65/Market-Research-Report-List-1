<p><ul><li>Solid NBR Rubber</li><li>NBR Latex</li><li>Hydrogenated NBR (HNBR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nitrile elastomers market consists of three primary types: Solid NBR Rubber, NBR Latex, and Hydrogenated NBR (HNBR). Solid NBR Rubber is a common choice for its excellent oil resistance and mechanical properties, widely used in automotive and industrial applications. NBR Latex, being a water-based option, offers flexibility and is often utilized in coatings and adhesives. Hydrogenated NBR (HNBR) enhances the stability against heat and ozone, making it ideal for demanding applications such as seals and gaskets in extreme environments.</p></p>
