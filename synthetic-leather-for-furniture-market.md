<p><p>Synthetic leather for furniture encompasses various types, each with unique properties. PVC (polyvinyl chloride) is durable and water-resistant but less breathable. Normal PU (polyurethane) offers a softer feel and is more environmentally friendly compared to PVC. Microfiber PU mimics the texture of natural leather while providing durability and breathability. Ecological function PU is designed with sustainability in mind, utilizing eco-friendly materials and processes. Each type serves specific design and functional needs in the furniture market.</p></p>
