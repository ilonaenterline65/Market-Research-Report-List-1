<p><p>Gas-Insulated Load Break Switches (GILBS) are critical electrical devices designed to handle load currents in high-voltage substations while minimizing space requirements. They utilize sulfur hexafluoride (SF6) gas as an insulating and arcing medium, which enables the switch to operate effectively under various environmental conditions. Their compact design and reliability make them a preferred choice for utilities and industrial applications, especially in urban areas where space is limited.</p><p>The Gas-Insulated Load Break Switch Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.3% during the forecast period.
