<p><p>The Miniature Voltage Transformer (MVT) market is categorized into two main types: for measurement and for protection. Measurement MVAs are designed to accurately transform high voltage levels into lower, manageable voltages for metering and monitoring applications, ensuring precise measurement in electrical systems. In contrast, protection MVAs are focused on safeguarding electrical equipment by detecting faults and providing necessary current to protective relays, thus ensuring system stability and safety. Both types are essential in electrical power infrastructure management.</p></p>
