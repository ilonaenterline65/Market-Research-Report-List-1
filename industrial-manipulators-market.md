<p><p>Industrial manipulators are categorized into pneumatic, electronic, and hydraulic types based on their actuation mechanisms. Pneumatic manipulators leverage compressed air for movement, offering flexibility and rapid operation, making them ideal for lightweight tasks. Electronic manipulators use electric motors and advanced controls, providing precise handling and programmability, suitable for complex automation. Hydraulic manipulators utilize fluid power to exert strong forces, excelling in heavy-duty applications where high lifting capacities are necessary. Each type serves diverse industrial needs based on efficiency and application requirements.</p></p>
