<p><strong>The Valve Gear Boxes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Worm Gear</li><li>Bevel Gear</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The valve gear boxes market is primarily categorized into two main types: worm gears and bevel gears. Worm gear boxes offer high torque and compact design, making them ideal for applications requiring significant speed reduction and precise control. On the other hand, bevel gears provide efficient power transmission at various angles, suitable for applications needing direction change without losing torque. Both types are crucial in industrial valve operations, contributing to enhanced performance, reliability, and efficiency in various mechanical systems.</p></p>
