<p><strong>The Activated Carbon Powders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Physical Method</li><li>Chemical Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The activated carbon powders market is broadly classified into two production methods: physical and chemical. The physical method involves carbonizing raw materials at high temperatures, followed by activation with gases like steam or carbon dioxide to enhance porosity. In contrast, the chemical method uses chemical agents, such as phosphoric acid or potassium hydroxide, to create a more advanced pore structure at lower temperatures. Both methods yield activated carbon with diverse applications, including water purification, air filtration, and industrial processes.</p></p>
