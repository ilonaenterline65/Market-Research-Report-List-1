<p><p>The Smart Cards Automated Fare Collection System encompasses various market types: Fareboxes, Ticket Vending Machines (TVMs), and Validators. Fareboxes are installed on transport vehicles, allowing passengers to deposit fare directly. TVMs enable users to purchase or reload smart cards at stations or designated locations, enhancing accessibility. Validators are used at entry and exit points, ensuring proper fare deduction and facilitating seamless travel. Together, these components streamline payment processes, improve efficiency, and enhance the passenger experience in public transportation systems.</p></p>
