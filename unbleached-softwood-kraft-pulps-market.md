<p><p>The unbleached softwood kraft pulps market comprises several types: Mechanical Pulp, Semi-Chemical Pulp, and Non-Wood Pulp. Mechanical Pulp is produced by grinding wood logs and is characterized by its high yield and lower quality, making it suitable for newsprint and paperboard. Semi-Chemical Pulp involves partial chemical treatment, offering a balance of strength and cost-efficiency, ideal for products like corrugated boxes. Non-Wood Pulp is derived from non-wood fibers such as straw or bamboo, providing an eco-friendly alternative with diverse applications in paper manufacturing.</p></p>
