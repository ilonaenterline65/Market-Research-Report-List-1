<p><p>The air purification machine market comprises three main types: Passive, Active, and Active-Passive Hybrid systems. Passive air purifiers rely on natural airflow to filter pollutants through materials like HEPA filters, typically without using electricity. Active air purifiers employ mechanical processes, such as ionization or UV light, to actively reduce airborne contaminants. Active-Passive Hybrid systems combine both techniques, utilizing active filtration methods alongside passive components to enhance air quality and effectiveness, catering to diverse consumer needs and environments.</p></p>
