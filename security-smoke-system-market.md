<p><p>The Security Smoke System market is primarily divided into two types: water-based and oil-based systems. Water-based systems utilize a mixture of water and specific additives to create a dense smoke, providing visibility obstruction to deter intruders. These systems are often perceived as safer due to their lower toxicity. In contrast, oil-based systems generate smoke through heated oils, offering a thicker and more persistent fog. While effective in security applications, oil-based systems may raise concerns regarding safety and environmental impact.</p></p>
