<p><strong>The Robotic Window Cleaners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vacuum Suction</li><li>Fan Adsorption</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The robotic window cleaners market comprises two main types: vacuum suction and fan adsorption. Vacuum suction models use powerful pumps to create a strong vacuum that firmly adheres to window surfaces, allowing them to navigate vertical panes effectively. In contrast, fan adsorption devices utilize high-velocity air fans to create a vacuum, allowing them to cling to windows as they clean. Both types offer unique advantages in efficiency and design, appealing to different consumer preferences and cleaning needs.</p></p>
