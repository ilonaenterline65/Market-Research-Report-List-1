<p><p>The rhombohedral boron nitride market primarily consists of two types: graphite structure boron nitride and diamond structure boron nitride. Graphite structure boron nitride features layered planes similar to graphite, offering lubricating properties and thermal stability, making it ideal for industrial applications. In contrast, diamond structure boron nitride exhibits superior hardness and thermal conductivity, making it suitable for cutting tools and abrasive applications. Both types serve distinct markets based on their unique properties, catering to varying industrial needs.</p></p>
