<p><ul><li>Round Slings</li><li>Web Slings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The synthetic lifting slings market is primarily categorized into round slings and web slings. Round slings are made from continuous loops of high-strength fibers encased in a protective sleeve, offering flexibility and load distribution for heavy lifting. Web slings, on the other hand, consist of woven straps that provide versatility in various lifting scenarios, often featuring reinforced eyes for durability. Both types are lightweight, resistant to damage from chemicals and UV exposure, enhancing safety in industrial applications.</p></p>
