Dynamic stabilization devices provide support to the spine without rigid fixation, allowing for natural movement. Artificial disc replacement devices replace damaged intervertebral discs, preserving mobility and reducing pain. Annulus repair devices focus on repairing the outer disc layer to prevent further degeneration. Nucleus disc prosthesis devices replace the inner disc material, supporting spinal functionality and improving patient outcomes while preserving natural movement.</p></p>
