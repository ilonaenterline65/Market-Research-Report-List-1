<p><ul><li>Bullet Cameras</li><li>PTZ Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The security thermal camera market primarily comprises two types: Bullet Cameras and PTZ (Pan-Tilt-Zoom) Cameras. Bullet cameras are fixed-position cameras designed for surveillance, typically mounted on walls or ceilings to monitor specific areas. They are compact and effective for perimeter security. In contrast, PTZ cameras offer flexible movement, allowing operators to pan, tilt, and zoom in on subjects, making them ideal for large areas requiring dynamic surveillance. Both types enhance security by providing thermal imaging capabilities, enabling visibility in low-light conditions.</p></p>
