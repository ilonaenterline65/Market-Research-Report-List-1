<p><p>The breast imaging equipment market comprises two primary types: analog and digital breast imaging equipment. Analog breast imaging utilizes traditional film-based methods, offering basic visualization of breast tissue, but is limited in resolution and diagnostic capabilities. In contrast, digital breast imaging equipment employs advanced technologies such as digital mammography and breast tomosynthesis, providing superior image quality, enhanced diagnostic accuracy, and the ability to store and share images electronically. This advancement facilitates better patient outcomes and streamlined workflows in healthcare settings.</p></p>
