<p><p>Hydro-Pumped Storage Plants (HPSPs) are an essential component of renewable energy infrastructure, facilitating grid stability and energy management by storing excess energy generated during low demand periods. These plants utilize two water reservoirs at different elevations; when power demand is low, excess electricity is used to pump water to the upper reservoir. During peak demand, the stored water is released to generate electricity, thus providing a reliable energy source.</p><p>The Hydro-Pumped Storage Plants Market is poised for significant growth, driven by an increasing demand for renewable energy, advancements in technology, and the need for energy storage solutions.
