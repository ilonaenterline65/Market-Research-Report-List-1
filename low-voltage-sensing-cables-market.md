<p><p>The Low Voltage Sensing Cables market features two primary types: single-mode fiber and multi-mode fiber. Single-mode fiber utilizes a single light path, allowing for long-distance data transmission with minimal signal loss, making it ideal for expansive applications. In contrast, multi-mode fiber supports multiple light paths, enabling higher data rates over shorter distances, suited for local area networks and smaller environments. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing communication, automation, and monitoring systems across various sectors.</p></p>
