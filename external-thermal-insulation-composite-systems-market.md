<p><p>External Thermal Insulation Composite Systems (ETICS) market comprises two main types: polymer-based and polymer-modified systems. Polymer-based ETICS consist of materials like expanded polystyrene or mineral wool, providing effective thermal insulation and moisture resistance. Polymer-modified systems, on the other hand, enhance traditional materials with polymers to improve adhesion, flexibility, and weather resistance, making them suitable for various climatic conditions. Both types play a crucial role in energy efficiency and building performance through effective insulation solutions.</p></p>
