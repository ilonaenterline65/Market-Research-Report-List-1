<p><ul><li>Optical Scanning</li><li>Vein Scanning</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The handprint scanner market can be divided into two main types: optical scanning and vein scanning. Optical scanning utilizes high-resolution cameras to capture the unique patterns of an individual's handprint, making it effective for access control and identity verification. In contrast, vein scanning analyzes the unique vascular patterns within the hand using infrared light, offering enhanced security due to its difficulty to replicate. Both technologies provide efficient biometric solutions for various industries, reinforcing identity management and access security.</p></p>
