<p><p>The constant wattage electric heating cable market is primarily divided into two types: parallel and series electric heating cables. Parallel electric heating cables distribute power uniformly along their length, allowing individual segments to operate independently, which enhances reliability and control. In contrast, series electric heating cables connect end-to-end, causing the entire length to heat based on the current through the series, resulting in potential temperature uniformity challenges. Both types cater to different applications in industrial, commercial, and residential heating.</p></p>
