<p><ul><li>Internal Electroceutical Devices</li><li>External Electroceutical Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electroceutical devices market is divided into two main types: internal and external electroceutical devices. Internal electroceutical devices are implanted within the body to regulate biological functions, often used for conditions like chronic pain or neurological disorders. External electroceutical devices, on the other hand, are applied on the body's surface to deliver therapeutic electrical stimulation, suitable for various treatments such as muscle rehabilitation and pain management. Both types leverage electrical signals to enhance or modulate bodily functions for therapeutic benefits.</p></p>
