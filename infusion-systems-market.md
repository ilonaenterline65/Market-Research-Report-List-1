<p><p>Infusion systems comprise various types designed to deliver fluids, medications, or nutrients to patients. General purpose or volumetric pumps ensure precise fluid administration, while patient-controlled analgesia pumps empower patients to manage their pain relief. Ambulatory pumps offer portability, enabling patients to receive treatment while on the move. Other infusion systems may include implantable pumps and syringe drivers catering to specific medical needs. Each type serves distinct clinical requirements, enhancing patient care and treatment efficacy.</p></p>
