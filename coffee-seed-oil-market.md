<p><p>The coffee seed oil market is primarily categorized into two types: cold-pressed oils and CO2/solvent extraction oils. Cold-pressed oils are obtained through a mechanical pressing process that retains the natural flavor and nutrients of the coffee seeds. In contrast, CO2 and solvent extraction oils involve using solvents or carbon dioxide under high pressure to extract oil, resulting in a more concentrated product. Both methods offer unique benefits, catering to various applications in cosmetics, food, and wellness industries.</p></p>
