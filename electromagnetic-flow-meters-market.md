<p><p>Electromagnetic flow meters are crucial for measuring the flow of conductive liquids. The Direct-Current (DC) type uses a constant voltage to create a magnetic field, allowing real-time flow measurements with high accuracy and stability. In contrast, the Induction type employs alternating current to generate the magnetic field, commonly used for larger pipes and higher flow rates. Both types are essential in various industries, providing reliable and efficient flow measurement solutions tailored to specific fluid characteristics and application requirements.</p></p>
