<p><p>The automotive horns market includes various types designed for different vehicle needs. **Air horns** use compressed air to produce loud, deep sounds, ideal for large vehicles like trucks and buses. **Electronic horns** rely on electronic circuits to generate sound, offering compact, efficient, and customizable tones for passenger cars. **Electromagnetic horns** utilize electromagnets and diaphragms to create sharp, attention-grabbing sounds, commonly used in motorcycles and smaller vehicles.
