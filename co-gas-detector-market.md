<p><p>The CO gas detector market is primarily divided into two types: wall/fixed detectors and portable detectors. They are designed for long-term use and are often connected to alarm systems. Portable detectors, on the other hand, are handheld devices that can be used in various locations, offering flexibility for temporary monitoring, especially in industrial settings or during outdoor activities. Both types are essential for ensuring safety and detecting harmful CO gas.</p></p>
