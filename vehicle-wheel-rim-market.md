<p><p>The vehicle wheel rim market is segmented into **tubeless wheel rims** and **tube wheel rims**. Tubeless rims are designed for airtight sealing, eliminating the need for inner tubes, offering better safety, fuel efficiency, and reduced maintenance. Tube rims require inner tubes to hold air, commonly used in older or heavy-duty vehicles. Tubeless rims dominate modern markets due to their advanced performance, while tube rims remain relevant in specific applications where durability and cost-effectiveness are prioritized. Both cater to diverse automotive needs.</p></p>
