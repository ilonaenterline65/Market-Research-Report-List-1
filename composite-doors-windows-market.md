<p><p>The composite doors and windows market includes various materials, notably Fiber Reinforced Plastics (FRP) and Wood Plastic Composites (WPC). FRP is known for its lightweight and high strength, offering excellent durability and resistance to weather and corrosion. It is ideal for modern designs. In contrast, WPC combines wood fibers with plastic, providing an aesthetically appealing, eco-friendly option that mimics natural wood while offering improved moisture resistance and lower maintenance. Both types cater to energy efficiency and sustainability demands in construction.</p></p>
