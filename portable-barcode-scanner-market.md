<p><strong>The Portable Barcode Scanner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Laser Scanner</li><li>Linear Imager Scanner</li><li>2D Imager Scanner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable barcode scanner market comprises three main types: Laser Scanners, Linear Imager Scanners, and 2D Imager Scanners. Laser Scanners utilize laser beams to read barcodes, offering high accuracy and speed, making them ideal for long-range scanning in retail and logistics. Linear Imager Scanners capture barcode images using a linear array, providing good performance for traditional one-dimensional barcodes. 2D Imager Scanners can read both 1D and 2D barcodes, allowing for greater versatility in applications such as inventory management and mobile payments.</p></p>
