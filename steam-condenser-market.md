<p><strong>The Steam Condenser Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Jet Condenser</li><li>Surface Condenser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steam condenser market includes two primary types: jet condensers and surface condensers. Jet condensers utilize the direct mixing of steam with cooling water, facilitating heat exchange and subsequent condensation. They are often simpler and more compact, making them suitable for smaller applications. On the other hand, surface condensers feature a separation between the steam and cooling water, promoting efficient heat transfer while minimizing water contamination. They are commonly used in large power plants due to their enhanced performance and reliability.</p></p>
