<p><p>Pleated membrane filtration is a sophisticated separation technology that utilizes pleated membranes to enhance the efficiency of filtration processes. Notably, the growing trend towards miniaturization in filtration systems is enhancing the appeal of pleated membranes, enabling higher performance in compact environments. <p><p>The pleated membrane filtration market comprises several types, each catering to specific filtration needs. PP filters, made from polypropylene, offer chemical resistance and are ideal for general-purpose filtration. PES filters, constructed from polyethersulfone, are known for their high flow rates and thermal stability, suitable for sterilization applications. PTFE filters provide excellent chemical resistance and are often used in aggressive environments. Nylon filters, known for their durability and versatility, are used in various liquid and gas filtration applications. Additionally, "Others" category includes specialized materials for niche applications.</p></p>
