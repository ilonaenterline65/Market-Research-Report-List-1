<p><p>The Shortwave Infrared (SWIR) camera market includes two primary types: SWIR Area Cameras and SWIR Linear Cameras. SWIR Area Cameras capture images over a two-dimensional field, making them suitable for applications requiring detailed scene analysis, such as industrial inspections and security surveillance. In contrast, SWIR Linear Cameras utilize a linear array of sensors to scan across a scene, ideal for scenarios like conveyor belt monitoring or aerial imaging, providing rapid data acquisition with high sensitivity to SWIR wavelengths.</p></p>
