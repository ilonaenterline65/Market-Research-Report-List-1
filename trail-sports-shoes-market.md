<p><p>Trail sports shoes can be categorized into lightweight and buffer market types. Lightweight trail shoes prioritize agility and speed, designed for experienced runners who seek minimal weight and enhanced breathability. These shoes are ideal for racing or fast-paced trail activities. In contrast, buffer market shoes emphasize cushioning and support, catering to a broader audience, including casual hikers and those seeking comfort over long distances. These provide stability and protection on rugged terrain, appealing to users who prioritize durability and comfort over speed.</p></p>
