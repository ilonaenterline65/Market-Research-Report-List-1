<p><p>The whitebox server market includes several key types: rack-mount servers, blade servers, and whole cabinet servers. Rack-mount servers are designed to fit into standard server racks, offering modularity and efficient space use. Blade servers are compact, thin units that share power and cooling resources within a chassis, enhancing density and scalability. Whole cabinet servers integrate all components within a complete cabinet, providing a turn-key solution for data centers needing high-density performance and simplified deployment. Each type caters to different operational needs and budgets.</p></p>
