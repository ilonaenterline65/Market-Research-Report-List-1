<p><strong>The Hand Extruders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Twin Screw Hand Extruders</li><li>Single Screw Hand Extruders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hand extruders market is primarily divided into two types: twin screw hand extruders and single screw hand extruders. Twin screw extruders utilize two intermeshing screws for enhanced mixing and processing, making them suitable for materials requiring thorough blending. They are often preferred for complex formulations. Single screw extruders, on the other hand, use a single screw for material transport and processing, offering simplicity and efficiency, ideal for less complex materials and applications. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
