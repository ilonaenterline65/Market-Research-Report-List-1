<p><p>The automobile generators market comprises AC and DC generators, each serving distinct functions. DC generators produce direct current, traditionally powering internal combustion engine vehicles by charging batteries and running electrical systems. Both types are integral to vehicle performance, with AC generators gaining traction in modern, energy-efficient designs, while DC generators remain relevant in conventional automotive applications.
