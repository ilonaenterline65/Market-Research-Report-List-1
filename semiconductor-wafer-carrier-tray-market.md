<p><p>The semiconductor wafer carrier tray market is primarily divided into two types: in-process wafer carrier trays and shipment wafer carrier trays. In-process wafer carrier trays are used during the manufacturing phase to hold wafers safely while they undergo various fabrication processes, ensuring minimal contamination and damage. In contrast, shipment wafer carrier trays are designed for the safe transportation of finished wafers to customers, providing protective features to prevent any physical or environmental harm during transit.</p></p>
