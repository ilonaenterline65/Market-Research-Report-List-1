<p><p>Thermal management hardware markets can be categorized into Type I and Type II. Type I markets focus on passive cooling solutions, such as thermal interface materials and heat sinks, which rely on natural heat dissipation methods. In contrast, Type II markets involve active cooling systems, including heat exchangers, fans, and liquid cooling systems that use energy to manage excess heat. Both types are essential for optimizing performance and reliability in various applications, including electronics, automotive, and industrial equipment.</p></p>
