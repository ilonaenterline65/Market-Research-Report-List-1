<p><ul><li>TTL Loupes (through the lens loupes)</li><li>Flip-up Loupes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Smart Medical Loupes market primarily consists of two types: TTL (Through-the-Lens) loupes and flip-up loupes. TTL loupes are custom-fitted to the user's prescription, providing a streamlined design and enhanced ergonomics, facilitating prolonged use without discomfort. In contrast, flip-up loupes are mounted on a frame and can be tilted up when not in use, offering flexibility and ease of storage. Both types enhance visual acuity for healthcare professionals, improving precision in medical procedures and examinations.</p></p>
