<p><p>Single-shaft shredders are classified into three market types based on their speed and application. Slow speed shredders operate at lower RPMs, providing efficient material reduction with minimal noise, ideal for sensitive materials. Medium speed shredders balance between power and throughput, suitable for a variety of materials, including plastics and wood. High speed shredders, operating at higher RPMs, offer rapid processing and are effective for lighter materials, maximizing production efficiency while generating finer outputs. Each type serves distinct industrial needs.</p></p>
