<p><p>The fingerprint recognition module market includes various types such as capacitive, optical, and RF fingerprint modules. Capacitive fingerprint modules use electrical capacitance to capture detailed fingerprint patterns, offering high accuracy and durability. Optical fingerprint modules utilize light to capture images of fingerprints, commonly found in smartphones. RF fingerprint modules employ radio frequency technology for capturing prints, often used in secure access applications. Other types may include acoustic and thermal fingerprint recognition technologies, each catering to specific use cases and applications.</p></p>
