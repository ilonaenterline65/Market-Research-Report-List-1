<p><p>The fruit harvester market comprises three main types: self-propelled, trailed, and mounted fruit harvesters. Self-propelled fruit harvesters are autonomous machines, offering mobility and efficiency in large orchards. Trailed fruit harvesters are towed by tractors, providing flexibility and ease of use with existing equipment. Mounted fruit harvesters are designed to be attached directly to tractors, allowing for compact operations in smaller fields. Each type caters to different farming scales, crop types, and operational needs.</p></p>
