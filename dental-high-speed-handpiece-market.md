<p><strong>The Dental High Speed Handpiece Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inside Exhaust Handpiece</li><li>Outside Exhaust Handpiece</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental high-speed handpiece market is categorized into two main types: inside exhaust handpieces and outside exhaust handpieces. Inside exhaust handpieces expel air and debris through the handpiece housing, offering a cleaner field of operation since the air circulates internally. Conversely, outside exhaust handpieces release air and debris externally, ensuring optimal visibility but potentially creating a less controlled environment. Both types serve the same purpose of enhancing dental procedures, yet their designs influence ergonomics and operational efficiency in clinical settings.</p></p>
