<p><p>The special animal feed market comprises three main types: compound feed, fodder, and forage. Compound feed is a blend of various ingredients formulated to meet specific nutritional needs of livestock, enhancing growth and productivity. Fodder refers to any plant material, such as grains and legumes, cultivated specifically for livestock consumption, providing essential nutrients. Forage consists of fibrous plant material, including grasses and legumes, which livestock graze on, playing a crucial role in their diet and overall health.</p></p>
