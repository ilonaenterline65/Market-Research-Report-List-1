<p><strong>The MRI System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Closed MRI Systems</li><li>Open MRI Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MRI system market is primarily divided into closed and open MRI systems. Closed MRI systems feature a cylindrical shape, providing high field strength and detailed images, making them suitable for a wide range of diagnostic applications. However, they may cause discomfort for claustrophobic patients. In contrast, open MRI systems have a more spacious design, reducing anxiety and accommodating larger patients, but typically offer lower image quality and resolution. The choice between the two depends on patient comfort and imaging requirements.</p></p>
