<p><p>The electrolyte additives market features several key types that enhance battery performance and stability. Vinylene carbonate (VC) acts as a solvent and a protective layer for electrodes. Fluoroethylene carbonate (FEC) improves cycle stability and reduces lithium plating. 1,3-Propane sultone enhances thermal stability and conductivity. Lithium bis(fluorosulfonyl)imide (LiFSI) improves ionic conductivity and safety. Other additives may include various solvents and compounds, each designed to optimize battery efficiency, longevity, and overall performance in energy storage applications.</p></p>
