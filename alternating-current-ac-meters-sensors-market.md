<p><p>The Alternating Current (AC) meters sensors market includes several key types: Hall-Effect current sensing utilizes magnetic fields to measure current flow, providing accurate readings without direct electrical connections. Giant Magneto Resistive (GMR) sensors leverage changes in electrical resistance in response to magnetic fields, enabling precise and compact current measurements. Traditional current sensing techniques typically involve shunt resistors or transformers to monitor current, offering reliable measurements but often at the expense of size and efficiency compared to newer technologies.</p></p>
