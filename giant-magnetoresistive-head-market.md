<p><p>The Giant Magnetoresistive (GMR) head market is primarily segmented into two types: Film Type and Needle Type. In contrast, Needle Type GMR heads feature a sharp, probe-like design, making them suitable for high-precision applications such as scanning probe microscopy and magnetic field sensing. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing data storage and retrieval efficiency in various industries.</p></p>
