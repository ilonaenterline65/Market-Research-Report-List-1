<p><p>The synthetic fatliquors market is categorized into three types: anionic, cationic, and non-ionic. Anionic fatliquors are typically used for their excellent emulsification and lubrication properties, making them suitable for leather processing. Cationic fatliquors offer better fixation and compatibility with positively charged materials, enhancing softness and flexibility in leather products. Non-ionic fatliquors are versatile, providing balanced properties without interacting aggressively with leather surfaces, ideal for a variety of applications, including textile and polymer industries.</p></p>
