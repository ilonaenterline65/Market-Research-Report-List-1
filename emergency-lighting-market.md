<p><p>The emergency lighting market comprises two primary types: self-contained power supply and centralized power supply systems. Self-contained power supply units have their own battery and light source, allowing for independent operation during power outages. They are often easy to install and maintain. In contrast, centralized power supply systems utilize a single backup power source to support multiple lights, promoting efficiency and simplified management but requiring more complex installation. Both types ensure safety and visibility during emergencies.</p></p>
