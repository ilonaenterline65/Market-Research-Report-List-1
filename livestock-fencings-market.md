<p><p>The livestock fencing market is divided into two main types: electrical and non-electrical fencing. Electrical fencing utilizes charged wires to deter animals, providing a psychological barrier that is often more cost-effective and easier to install over large areas. Non-electrical fencing includes traditional materials like wood, barbed wire, and welded wire, offering physical barriers without electrical components. Each type serves distinct purposes depending on the livestock species, terrain, and intended level of security, catering to various agricultural needs.</p></p>
