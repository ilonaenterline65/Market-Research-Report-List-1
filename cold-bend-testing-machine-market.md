<p><strong>The Cold Bend Testing Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>3-Point Bend</li><li>4-Point Bend</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Cold Bend Testing Machine market is segmented into 3-Point Bend and 4-Point Bend testing types. The 3-Point Bend test applies a load at a single midpoint, allowing for the evaluation of a material's flexural strength and elasticity with a simpler setup. In contrast, the 4-Point Bend test uses two load points, creating a more uniform bending moment over the specimen's length, providing a comprehensive assessment of material performance under stress, making it suitable for more in-depth analysis.</p></p>
